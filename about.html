About Chris Binnie

Chris Binnie is a cloud native security consultant and author with almost 30 years of experience working with critical online infrastructure. Based in Edinburgh, Scotland, he specialises in cloud native security, Kubernetes security, Linux server hardening, and DevSecOps practices.

As a recognised expert in cybersecurity, Chris has authored multiple technical books and has been a regular contributing writer for Linux Magazine and ADMIN Magazine for around 15 years. His practical experience spans from founding a colocation business in 2001 with zero-downtime architecture across two data centres, to designing and building a cloud-based media streaming platform in 2012 that served HD video to 77 countries for international sports events and music festivals.
